About Us After years in the commercial fishing industry, Pat felt he needed a change of pace. His long months at sea were taxing on his body, so he took up baking with his family (pictures left) as a hobbie. As a born-and-raised Seattlite, he had a deep love for coffee and coffee-time confections. As a career fisherman, he also had a deep love for Salmon. He decided to combine these two things and, thus, Pat's Salmon Cookies were born. Our slogan is "there's nothing fishy about these cookies" because we use sustainably sourced fish and organic ingredients. Along with introducing the world to his new recipe, he wanted to produce a product that was green, clean and sustainable. We currently have locations in 5 major cities, but expansion is in the works! We also have salmon cookies merch in the form of t-shirts and cookie cutters that'll make the perfect gift for your friends and family!
